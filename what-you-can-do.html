Of course, after hearing all of these terrible things, you might want to contribute to stopping the dolphin abuse in dolphinariums. How can you do that, though? Well, here are some simple ways you can aid in this fight:

  1. Donate!
  2. Share this with friends!
  3. Don't go to dolphin shows, even if it's in another country!
  4. Sign petitions!
  5. Speaking of petitions, you can start your own!
  6. Buy from the Dolphin Project store!
